## Quick Setup üöÄ

1. Follow [this link](https://discord.com/api/oauth2/authorize?client_id=903352344941588480&permissions=53496832&scope=bot%20applications.commands) to connect the bot to your Discord server.
2. Type `/` in any text channel to see what commands are available with the bot!

### Intro Theme Song

1. To set your intro theme song, use `/set <YouTube link> <duration>` in a text channel.

   The `<duration>` is optional, and can be any integer or decimal value between 1 and 20 seconds.
1. Join a voice channel and your theme song will play for the given duration!

### Outro Theme Song (NEW)

1. To set your outro song, use `/set-outro <YouTube link> <duration>` in a text channel.
1. To trigger your outro, use `/outro` and your outro will be played, then you'll be disconnected from the voice channel!
